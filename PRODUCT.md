# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Brazilian entrepreneurs, digital creators, and infoprodutors. They operate in the digital marketing space, selling courses, templates, toolkits, and guides. They are action-oriented, results-driven, and respond to urgency. Their context is mobile-first, often browsing on phones during breaks or while multitasking.

## Product Purpose

A high-converting sales page for low-ticket digital products. The page exists to drive immediate purchases through urgency, social proof, and aggressive CTAs. Success looks like: visitor lands, sees the offer, feels the urgency, and clicks the CTA button within 30 seconds.

## Brand Personality

Bold, urgent, action-oriented. The voice is confident and slightly aggressive, pushing the visitor toward action. Not timid, not polite-to-a-fault. Think: a direct, no-nonsense friend who tells you exactly what you need to do and why you should do it NOW.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS templates (Inter font everywhere, purple-to-blue gradients, cards nested in cards)
- AI slop aesthetics (gray text on colored backgrounds, bounce easing, gradient text, glassmorphism as default)
- Corporate/enterprise style (stiff, formal, overly polished to the point of being sterile)
- The 2023-era "tiny uppercase tracked eyebrow above every section" pattern
- Numbered section markers as default scaffolding (01 / 02 / 03)

## Design Principles

1. **Urgency is king** — every element should push toward the CTA, never away from it
2. **Social proof is oxygen** — testimonials, numbers, and proof must be visible and immediate
3. **Mobile-first, always** — design for the 768px viewport first, then scale up
4. **Bold over clever** — clarity beats sophistication; direct beats poetic
5. **Visual rhythm through spacing** — vary padding and margins to create breathing room and guide the eye

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Standard contrast ratios (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text). Keyboard navigation support. Consider reduced motion for animations. Portuguese language content with clear, readable typography.
